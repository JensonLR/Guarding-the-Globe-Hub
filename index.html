⚡ PRO TIPS & TRICKS ⚡

📈 GDA Ops Tips

  • Prioritize GDA Ops for progression and resources.
  • Use faction synergies for better performance.
  • Place ranged/flyers to reduce downtime.
  • Prioritize event ops for rare resources.

🦸‍♂️ Hero Management

  • Recruit elite heroes for better stats and skills.
  • Level up multiple versions for ops farming.
  • Fuse duplicates as fodder for upgrades.

⚙️ Gear and Artifacts

  • Manage gear with a trickle-down strategy.
  • Target epic-ranked gear for reinforcements.
  • Equip artifacts for special abilities in combat.

💰 Shops and Resources

  • Level up shops for better offers.
  • Avoid gem refreshes except for Artifact mats.
  • Hoard resources for 5 pulls in The Lab.

🌟 Agent Level and Alliances

  • Agent level impacts unlocking new ops.
  • Alliances offer idle rewards; no major difference solo vs. group.
  • Tap the screen in Alliance tab to fire a laser (fun tip).
