Battle Tactics for New Recruits

In the fight to guard the globe, every hero must hone their skills. Whether you’re facing street-level crooks or the looming threat of Viltrumite forces, mastering the main missions is your first step. Earn XP, upgrade your gear, and rank up your high-elite heroes. Remember: a hero’s resolve is forged in the heat of battle—stay sharp, and never back down.

Squad Synergy & Team Building

Building the ultimate team means more than just assembling the strongest heroes. Strategically position your heavy hitters in the rear for maximum damage, deploy steadfast defenders upfront to absorb enemy blows, and integrate support heroes to keep your squad in peak fighting condition. Whether you’re rolling with GDA-trained elites or renegade Criminals, every role counts. Adapt your team to counter specific threats and embrace the unpredictability of cosmic battles.
